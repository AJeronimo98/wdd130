Temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Oaxaca Mexico Temple

About the Temple

The Oaxaca Mexico Temple was the 74th operating temple worldwide and the 8th in Mexico. It serves members throughout Southern Mexico.

Dedicated March 11, 2000: Dedicatory Prayer (English)

Arrival and Local Information

The area around the temple is known for its quiet, yet accessible location near the Reforma neighborhood.

  • **Visitor Center:** There is no dedicated visitors' center on site, but patrons are welcome to walk the beautiful temple grounds.
  • **Accommodation:** No temple housing is available. Several lodging options are available in the surrounding area.
  • **Nearby Shopping/Markets:**
    • **Markets (Centro Histórico):** The Zócalo area and nearby markets like **Benito Juárez Market** are the main commercial hubs, easily reachable by car or public transport.
    • **Local Shops:** Smaller commercial areas are available in the Reforma neighborhood near the temple.
