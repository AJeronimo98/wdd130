The Family Float (Half-Day)

Perfect for beginners and young families, this half-day trip covers a scenic stretch of Class I and II rapids. Enjoy gentle currents, abundant wildlife, and a perfect introduction to river running. Ages 6+. Includes light snacks and all safety equipment.

Grand Canyon Run (3 Days)

An exhilarating multi-day experience covering challenging Class III and IV rapids. You'll camp on sandy beaches under the stars, guided by our experienced team. This trip is for ages 16+ and requires a moderate fitness level. All meals and professional photography included.

The Adrenaline Rush (Full-Day)

This is our most popular full-day trip, featuring non-stop action on Class IV rapids. Designed for experienced rafters looking for a serious thrill. You must be 18+ and confident in the water.
