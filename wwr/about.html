Adventure Expeditions

Our mission is to connect people with nature through thrilling, safe, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences. We believe that true adventure is found where the river meets the soul. We are committed to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating lifelong memories for every guest.

History

Founded in 1985 by a group of passionate river guides, Adventure Expeditions started with a single raft and a dream to share the excitement of the rapids. Over the decades, we’ve grown into a leading rafting outfitter, but our core values—respect for the river and joy in the journey—remain unchanged. We operate on the highest safety standards and continuously train our team to ensure the best possible experience for both beginners and seasoned adventurers.
